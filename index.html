Do Video Games Cause Violence?

Many people believe that playing violent video games can make a person violent in real life. When a tragedy happens, people often look at the suspect's gaming habits first. They quickly assume that virtual violence leads directly to physical violence. However, scientific research does not support this simple explanation. There is no clear evidence that video games alone cause violent behavior. Millions of teenagers play action-packed video games every day, but the vast majority of them never hurt anyone.

Tacloban School Shooting


The School Shooting Investigation in Tacloban City A tragic school shooting happened at San Jose National High School in Tacloban City in June 2026. The incident resulted in casualties and injured several students. During the investigation, the police looked into many possible reasons why the young suspects committed the crime. The investigators checked the digital history of the young suspects. They found out that the suspects spent a lot of time playing online video games. They also joined various online gaming group chats. However, investigators and experts did not conclude that video games alone caused the violent attack. They explained that playing video games is not enough to make someone commit a real-world crime.
